ABOUT

Hello! My name is Andrey Khomenko, known as Trashslamdunk. I am a sound designer and beatmaker. I am 19 years old. I started my work in 2021 and have already collaborated with such artists as Lovv66, 163onmyneck, lil Morty, doomee, malenkiy yarche and many others.

In 2022, I participated in a sound design project for OSAI's table tennis AI. I have experience in creating sound design and feel new trends, as well as create them myself.
