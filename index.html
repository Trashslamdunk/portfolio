ABOUT

Hello! My name is Andrii Khomenko, also known as Trashslamdunk. I am a sound designer and beatmaker, currently 20 years old. I began my journey in 2021 and have already had the opportunity to collaborate with notable artists such as Lovv66, 163onmyneck, lil Morty, doomee, malenkiy yarche, and many others. Notably, my beats have been featured on the upcoming albums "Grow Guide 2" by 163onmyneck and "D1" by doomee.

In 2022, I had the privilege to contribute to a sound design project for OSAI's table tennis AI. Additionally, I work as a sound designer for the YouTube channel Переговорка. With my experience in sound design, I am constantly attuned to emerging trends while also creating innovative sounds myself.
